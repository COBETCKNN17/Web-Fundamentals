The Copenhagen Experience

In November of 2016, I found a temporary job opportunity to work for Unity Technologies in Copenhagen, Denmark. The job was to do their Salesforce CPQ implementation. I accepted the opportunity to work for Unity. At first, the contract I signed was for 3 months. Luckily for me, it was extended 3 times, ultimately allowing me to live and work in Copenhagen through the end of 2017. I loved the city, its architecture, the bicycle lifestyle and its music scene.
